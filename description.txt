The tVoice Mod allows you and your friends to chat via voice directly in Terraria, with no need for any third-party software!
Currently, it only supports basic voice chat features (similar to those in CSGO), but more features will be added in the future, such as sound attenuation.

Some of this mod's features:
- Background Music Ducking, which automatically lowers the volume of the game's background music when someone is speaking.
- Proximity Chat, which introduces a positional effect to voices, and the closer the player, the louder the voice.

How to use:
1. Subscribe to this Mod
2. Enter the game, go to "Controls", scroll to the bottom, and set a keybind for "Voice Chat"
3. By default, press and hold the key to speak, and your friends will be able to hear you
4. You can set more custom options in the mod config

Notes:
- Voice chat will cause a certain network load, and if your network is poor, it may cause latency issues.
- The voice quality of this mod does not outperform professional third-party voice software, such as Teamspeak, Discord, etc.

泰拉语音聊天模组，允许你和朋友们直接通过泰拉语音聊天，不需要任何第三方软件！
目前仅支持基础的语音聊天功能（如果你玩过CSGO，跟那里面的语音功能基本类似）
后续会加入更多功能，如声音衰减功能：将玩家语音直接映射到游戏世界中，玩家离得越近声音就越大

这个Mod相比语音软件有以下优点：
- 音乐音量自动调节，当有人在说话时，自动降低游戏背景音乐的音量
- 空间语音，玩家离得越近声音就越大

使用方法：
1. 订阅此Mod
2. 进入游戏，进入“控件”，拉到底下，为“语音按键”设置一个快捷键
3. 默认情况下，按住此快捷键说话，你的好友就能听到
4. 你可以在模组配置中设置更多自定义选项

注：
- 语音聊天会导致一定网络负担，如果你的网络不好，可能会造成游戏卡顿
- 本模组的语音质量比不上专业的第三方语音软件，如Teamspeak、Discord等
